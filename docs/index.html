Welcome to the official documentation of kRPC2

What is kRPC2?

kRPC 2 will be the continuation of the original kRPC mod, but for KSP 2!

We aim to keep all of kRPC's features, and improve on the original.

When will kRPC2 be released?

It is at this point in time not possible to say when kRPC 2 will be released. We are waiting for the KSP 2 API to be released in order to start development.

Who is developing kRPC 2?

kRPC 2 was initiated by KozmoKode. The author of the original kRPC mod, djungelorm is also part of the development team for kRPC 2.

How can I come in contact with the kRPC 2 developers?

You can get in touch with us via our discord server.
